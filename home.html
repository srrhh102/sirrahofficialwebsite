My name is Sirrah Harris and I am a graphic design. My goal is to use my skills to make the world a better and safer place for people of color by bringing awareness to the issues involving our community. Thank you for visiting my website and be sure to also check out my social media pages.

Description
Happy Days is a web comic series I have created to spread awareness of Black Maternity Mortality here in the US. According to the National Library of Medicine, “Black women are at least three times more likely to die due to a pregnancy-related cause when compared to White women.” I hope to get involved in the conversation on solutions and inspire more people to do the same.
